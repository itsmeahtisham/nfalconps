ABOUT
FALCON EDUCATION SYSTEM

That the present education system is hardly adequate to equip the students to face the challenges of a fast changing modern world is an undeniable fact. The N.F.P School was convinced that radical changes have to be effected in the teaching methodology and course contents so that the system of education is not merely information based but value oriented, formulated to bring about transformation and maturity in the personality of the students. Thus in millennium year 2000, N.F.P.S started the ideal School of Qayyumabad, to rejuvenate the existing education system. The school, recognized by the Ministry of Education, Recognized by the Board of Secondary Education, The school has attained unparalleled status and stability in Qayyumabad with its own campus. It is gratifying to note that the school stands as a symbol of excellence, fulfilling the dreams of its founding fathers. The current and maximum strength of the school is 600. The staff comprising 25 teaching and 10 non-teaching staff including transport section not only fulfill the academic needs of the students but also take care of the all-round development of their personality.
